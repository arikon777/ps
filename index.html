Recipe for a winning Negroni:

Ingredients:

  1. Chunk
  2. Low-Ball
  3. 30 ml Campari
  4. 30 ml Rosso
  5. 30 ml Gin
  6. Stiring Glass
  7. long Spoon
  8. orange Twist
  9. Ice

Preparation: Mix all the ingredients together in a mixing cup 24 rounds with a long spoon(except for the orange twist), pour into the glass with the chunk and put an orange twist.
